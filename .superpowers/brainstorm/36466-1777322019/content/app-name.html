What should we call this?

Pick the name that feels right. Think about how it sounds, what it evokes, and how it reads as a CLI command.

Cortex
$ cortex add "MCP servers"

Cortex

The brain's outer layer — where processing, understanding, and connections happen. Clean, modern, authoritative. Easy to type, memorable. "I added it to my Cortex."

Synapse
$ synapse add "MCP servers"

Synapse

The connection between neurons — where knowledge transfers. Emphasizes the graph/relationship aspect. Slightly longer to type but very evocative. "Let me check my Synapse."

Nexus
$ nexus add "MCP servers"

Nexus

A central point where things connect. Short, punchy, easy to type. Has a tech/sci-fi feel. "It's all in my Nexus."

Dendrite
$ dendrite add "MCP servers"

Dendrite

Neural branches that receive and integrate information — exactly what this tool does. Most unique name, strong science identity. "I dendrite'd that concept."
